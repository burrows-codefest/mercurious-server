The History of Mercurious

Mercurious - liable to sudden unpredictable change; "erratic behavior"; "fickle weather"; "mercurial twists of temperament"; "a quicksilvercharacter, cool and willful at one moment, utterly fragile the next"

Mercurious was first forged on May 25, 2014 as the result of a skein of Front End developers wishing to learn more about the MEAN Stack (Mongo, Express, Angular and Node).

The initial release was built to improve communication across the department, concatenating different feeds into one source of easily viewable information.

Although created for good, Mercurious slowly turned into a bitter informal complaints system and a way to apologise when you released gases into the air at a slightly less than opportune time.

Planned future releases include a code review system and a reincarnation of the previously successful Bunomatic utility.
